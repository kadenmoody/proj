Rock Climbing

Introduction

Rock climbing: the GOAT of anything. Second to none, even LeBron. If you have not tried it out, I strongly recommend it. It is by far my favorite activity and I cannot get sick of it. It is a great way to get in shape and have fun at the same time. I have been climbing for about 4 years now and I am still learning new things every day. I have met so many amazing people through climbing and I am so grateful for all the experiences I have had. I have traveled and competed in competitions and loved every second of it. If you are looking for a new hobby or just something to do on the weekends, I highly recommend giving rock climbing a try.
